Set over 472 hectares, Chasewater Country Park supports a mosaic of habitats, including wetlands and lowland heath. In 1988, these nationally important habitats led to the Chasewater Heaths area being designated as a Site of Special Scientific Interest (SSSI). In 2011, this was extended to include almost the entire Country Park, plus the 90-hectare reservoir and nearby canal.
